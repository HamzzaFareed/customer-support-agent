NovaSaaS - Troubleshooting Guide

ISSUE 1: Cannot log in to my account
Symptoms: Login page shows an error or spins indefinitely.
Steps to fix:
1. Make sure you are using the correct email address associated with your account.
2. Try resetting your password via the "Forgot password" link on the login page.
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again.
4. Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers) and retry.
5. Try a different browser or incognito/private mode.
6. If your account was created via Google or Microsoft SSO, use the "Continue with Google/Microsoft" button instead of email/password.
7. If none of the above work, contact support@novasaas.com with your email address.

ISSUE 2: Email verification link not arriving
Symptoms: No verification email received after signing up or changing email.
Steps to fix:
1. Check your spam or junk folder.
2. Search your inbox for "noreply@novasaas.com".
3. Wait up to 5 minutes — delivery can be delayed.
4. Click "Resend verification email" on the confirmation screen.
5. Make sure your email provider is not blocking novasaas.com. Add noreply@novasaas.com to your safe senders list.
6. Try signing up with a different email address (Gmail works best).

ISSUE 3: App is slow or not loading properly
Symptoms: Pages take a long time to load, content does not appear, or the app freezes.
Steps to fix:
1. Check your internet connection and run a speed test.
2. Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac).
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
4. Disable browser extensions one by one to find a conflict.
5. Try a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).
6. Check the NovaSaaS status page at status.novasaas.com for any ongoing incidents.
7. If using the mobile app, force-close it and reopen. Try uninstalling and reinstalling if the issue persists.

ISSUE 4: File upload is failing
Symptoms: Files fail to upload or get stuck at a certain percentage.
Steps to fix:
1. Check the file size. Free plan is limited to 25MB per file. Pro plan allows up to 250MB per file.
2. Check that your workspace has not exceeded its total storage limit. Go to Settings > Storage to review.
3. Make sure the file type is supported. NovaSaaS supports: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PNG, JPG, GIF, MP4, ZIP.
4. Try uploading from a different browser or the desktop app.
5. If uploading via API, confirm your API token has write permissions.
6. Contact support if upload fails consistently for files within the size and type limits.

ISSUE 5: Team members not receiving invite emails
Symptoms: Invited users say they never got the invite link.
Steps to fix:
1. Ask the invitee to check their spam folder for an email from noreply@novasaas.com.
2. Go to Settings > Team and confirm the invite shows as "Pending" — if it shows "Accepted" they may already have an account.
3. Cancel the existing invite and resend it.
4. Make sure you entered the correct email address for the invitee.
5. If the invitee uses a company email, their IT/security policies may be blocking the email. Try inviting a personal email address to test.

ISSUE 6: Notifications are not working
Symptoms: No email or in-app notifications received for task updates, mentions, or due dates.
Steps to fix:
1. Go to Settings > Notifications and confirm notifications are enabled for the relevant events.
2. Check that the correct email address is listed under Settings > Profile.
3. Check your spam folder for notification emails from notifications@novasaas.com.
4. For in-app notifications, make sure your browser allows notifications from novasaas.com (check browser site settings).
5. On mobile, go to your device Settings > NovaSaaS > Notifications and ensure they are enabled.
6. If using Slack integration, re-authenticate the Slack connection under Settings > Integrations.
