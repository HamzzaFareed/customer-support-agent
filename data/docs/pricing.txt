FREQUENTLY ASKED BILLING QUESTIONS

Q: Can I switch plans at any time?
A: Yes. You can upgrade immediately — the new features are available right away and billing is prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfer for annual Enterprise contracts.

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first payment on any paid plan. After 7 days, refunds are issued on a case-by-case basis. Contact support@novasaas.com to request one.

Q: Is there a discount for nonprofits or students?
A: Yes. Nonprofits and educational institutions receive 50% off the Pro plan. Email verify@novasaas.com with proof of eligibility to apply.

Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade to Free?
A: Your data is preserved for 30 days after downgrading. Projects beyond the Free plan limit will be archived (read-only). You can reactivate them by upgrading again.
